Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

Runzo collects the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, and profile data
  • Location Data: GPS coordinates during run tracking to calculate distance and routes
  • Health & Fitness Data: Running workouts, training plans, and performance metrics
  • Device Information: Device type and identifiers for notifications
  • Payment Information: Processed securely through Stripe (we do not store payment details)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide personalized training plans and recommendations
  • Track and analyze your running performance
  • Send notifications about your workouts and progress
  • Process payments and manage subscriptions
  • Improve our services and user experience

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Database and authentication services
  • Stripe: Payment processing (payment data is handled by Stripe, not stored by us)
  • Google Maps: Location services and mapping

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure authentication and authorization
  • Regular security audits and updates

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data
  • Opt out of certain data collection

To exercise these rights, please contact us at support@runzo.app
